Our Proud Journey

SRI GOWRI GANESHA GELEYARA BALAGA proudly marks 13 years of celebrating Ganesh Chaturthi with devotion and unity. Over the years, our celebration has grown from a small gathering into a vibrant community festival filled with faith, culture, and joy.

Every year, we come together to welcome Lord Ganesha, perform aartis, organize cultural events, and strengthen the bonds within our community. This journey reflects our dedication and devotion.

Festival Schedule

Ganesh Sthapana

Installation of Lord Ganesha idol and opening prayers.

Maha Aarti

Special aarti and devotional prayers offered to Lord Ganesha.

Cultural Programs

Dance, music, and traditional cultural performances.

Annadanam

Community food distribution for devotees and visitors.

Grand Visarjan

Final procession and immersion ceremony.
